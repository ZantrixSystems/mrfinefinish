Meet Mohammed

A local tradesman you deal with directly from start to finish.

Hi, I'm Mohammed, the owner of Mr Fine Finish. When you get in touch, you deal directly with me. There are no middlemen and no big company layers, just clear communication with the person responsible for the work.

I take on a wide range of jobs, including painting and decorating, plastering, bricklaying and general home improvements. I work with homeowners and landlords, whether it's a one-off project or ongoing property maintenance, and every job is completed neatly, reliably and with pride.

I know having work done at home or in a rental property can feel stressful, so I keep things straightforward. I turn up on time, keep the workspace tidy and focus on getting the job done properly. My aim is simple: honest work, fair pricing and happy customers.

Most of my work comes through recommendations, which says a lot about the trust people place in Mr Fine Finish.
